Trees

Trees are essential to life on Earth. They provide us with oxygen, clean air and water, food, shelter, and medicine. Trees also play a vital role in the ecosystem, providing habitat for wildlife, preventing soil erosion, and regulating the climate.

Types

There are many different types of trees, classified in a variety of ways. One common classification is based on the type of leaf

Broadleaf trees :These trees have broad, flat leaves. Some examples of broadleaf trees include oak, maple, birch, and beech trees.

Coniferous trees :These trees have needle-like or scale-like leaves. Some examples of coniferous trees include pine, fir, spruce, and cedar trees.
